----------------------- REVIEW --------------------

Main contribution:
The article presents a well made overview of recent web information extraction systems and their taxonomy. The semantic annotation of web content is sketched as well and two approaches for web information extraction are proposed. The first is based on structural similarity in web pages (e.g. tables in web shops). The second is based on deep linguistic analysis and it should use a rule-based method for extraction of information from text.

Positive aspects:
The paper deals with a particularly topical problem.
Rich references.
The style of writing.

Negative aspects:
The suggested solutions are not verified, a work in progress is presented.
The introduced abbreviations should be used consistently.
Spelling errors (e.g. in 2.2) have to be corrected.
